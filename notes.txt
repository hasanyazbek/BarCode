Here’s a solid, practical feature list for a **Local Community Board** (think neighborhood app, housing society portal, campus board, or town forum). I’ll break it down from **core → advanced**, so you can pick what fits your project.

---

## Core Features (Must-Have)

### 1. User Accounts & Profiles

* Sign up / login (email, phone, or social)
* Basic profile (name, photo, area/block)
* Role types: Resident, Admin, Moderator

### 2. Community Posts

* Create posts (text, images, attachments)
* Categories: Announcements, Lost & Found, Events, Help Needed, For Sale
* Edit / delete own posts

### 3. Announcements

* Admin-only pinned announcements
* Expiry date for notices
* Highlighted or featured posts

### 4. Comments & Replies

* Comment on posts
* Threaded replies
* Edit / delete own comments

### 5. Events Board

* Create community events
* Event details (date, time, location)
* RSVP / Interested / Going

### 6. Search & Filters

* Search by keyword
* Filter by category, date, or popularity

---

## Engagement & Interaction Features

### 7. Reactions & Voting

* Like / Upvote posts
* Downvote (optional)
* Reaction emojis

### 8. Notifications

* New announcements
* Comments on your posts
* Event reminders

### 9. Polls & Surveys

* Create polls (admin or residents)
* Single / multiple choice
* View results in real time

### 10. Tags & Mentions

* Tag topics (#roadrepair, #pets)
* Mention users (@john)

---

## Moderation & Safety

### 11. Content Moderation

* Report posts or comments
* Admin review queue
* Auto-flag keywords

### 12. Role & Permission Management

* Admin, Moderator, Member permissions
* Approval workflow for posts (optional)

### 13. Verified Members

* Address or invite-code verification
* “Verified Resident” badge

---

## Community Utility Features

### 14. Lost & Found

* Dedicated section
* Status: Lost / Found / Resolved
* Location and date tracking

### 15. Local Services Directory

* Plumbers, electricians, tutors
* Ratings and reviews
* Contact info

### 16. Buy / Sell / Free Stuff

* Marketplace-style listings
* Price, condition, images
* Mark as Sold / Given Away

### 17. Emergency Alerts

* Admin emergency posts
* SMS / push notifications
* High-priority banner

---

## Admin & Analytics

### 18. Admin Dashboard

* User management
* Post moderation
* Engagement stats

### 19. Reports & Insights

* Active users
* Most discussed topics
* Event participation

### 20. Content Scheduling

* Schedule announcements or events
* Auto-expire posts

---

## Advanced / Nice-to-Have Features

### 21. Location-Based Feeds

* Block / street-wise posts
* Nearby communities

### 22. File & Document Sharing

* Meeting minutes
* Rules & guidelines
* Download history

### 23. Integration

* Google Maps for events
* Calendar sync
* WhatsApp / Email sharing

### 24. Mobile & Accessibility

* Responsive design
* Dark mode
* Multilingual support

---

## Optional Future Enhancements

* AI spam detection
* Sentiment analysis for community health
* Chat or private messaging
* Volunteer coordination module
